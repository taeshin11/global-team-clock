Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 1, 2026

1. Overview

Global Team Clock ("we", "our", "the service") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard information when you use our timezone overlap dashboard.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Automatically Collected

  • Browser timezone: Detected via the Intl API to auto-add your local timezone
  • Language preference: Detected via navigator.language for i18n

2.2 Locally Stored Data

  • Language preference: Stored in localStorage
  • Visitor counter: Anonymous visit count stored in localStorage

2.3 URL Parameters

When you share a link, your city selections and work hour settings are encoded in the URL. This data is not sent to our servers.

3. What We Do NOT Collect

  • Personal information (name, email, phone)
  • IP addresses
  • Location data beyond timezone
  • Cookies for tracking
  • Cross-site tracking data

4. Third-Party Services

4.1 Google AdSense

We use Google AdSense to display advertisements. Google may use cookies and web beacons to serve ads based on your visits to this and other websites. You can opt out of personalized advertising at Google Ads Settings.

4.2 Adsterra

We may use Adsterra ad network for additional ad placements. Adsterra may collect anonymous usage data for ad targeting purposes.

4.3 Vercel

Our website is hosted on Vercel. Vercel may collect standard web server logs including IP addresses and request data as part of their hosting service.

5. Data Storage

All application data (city selections, work hours, language preference) is stored locally in your browser. We do not maintain any server-side database of user data.

6. Children's Privacy

Our service is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
