About Global Team Clock

What is Global Team Clock?

Global Team Clock is a free, visual timezone overlap dashboard designed specifically for remote teams. It helps you find the best overlapping work hours across multiple cities and timezones, making it easier to schedule meetings, standups, and collaborative work sessions.

Why We Built This

As remote work becomes the norm, teams are increasingly distributed across the globe. Coordinating across timezones is one of the biggest challenges remote teams face. We built Global Team Clock to make this process visual, intuitive, and fast.

Key Features

  • Add up to 10 cities and see their timezones side by side
  • Visual 24-hour timeline bars with day/night indicators
  • Adjustable working hours per city
  • Automatic overlap detection and highlighting
  • Smart "Find Best Meeting Time" algorithm
  • Share your configuration via URL
  • Available in 8 languages
  • Works entirely in your browser - no data sent to servers

Privacy First

Global Team Clock runs entirely in your browser using the built-in JavaScript Intl API. No timezone data is sent to any server. Your city selections and work hour preferences are stored only in your browser's URL and local storage.

Technology

Built with vanilla HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. Uses the native Intl.DateTimeFormat and Intl.supportedValuesOf('timeZone') APIs for accurate timezone data. No external API calls needed.
